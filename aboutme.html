Olar! I’m a UX/UI Designer with an empathic and pragmatic approach to design. My curiosity about the development process of digital products brought me to Lisbon to start a postgrad in Digital Experience Design and explore the UX universe ✧.

What interests me the most in UX design is being all about the ride! Getting to learn more about people and how to translate all findings into friendly and fun experiences.

I look forward to collaborating on more projects focusing on user research and learning different methods that can help achieve user-centred solutions.
